Oobleck Fluid - Kannada

Oobleck is a fascinating, "magic" substance made from just cornstarch and water that seems to break the rules of science by acting like both a solid and a liquid.

What this video teaches

What is it? Oobleck is a "non-Newtonian fluid," meaning its thickness changes based on how much pressure you apply to it. The "Magic" Trick: If you punch Oobleck or squeeze it quickly, it feels as hard as a brick. But if you dip your fingers in slowly, it feels like gooey, runny water. Fun Origin: Its funny name comes from the Dr. Seuss book, Bartholomew and the Oobleck, where a sticky green slime falls from the sky.
