Underwater Volcano Experiment

A fun experiment to understand about Underwater Volcanoes and their formation.

What this video teaches

An underwater volcano experiment for kids uses hot, colored water in a small container inside a larger container of cold water to show how less-dense hot water rises, creating a lava-like eruption, demonstrating density and convection currents, which explains how real underwater volcanoes form islands over time by building up lava layers. It's a simple, visual way to teach that heat makes water expand and float, just like real magma rising from the Earth's mantle.
